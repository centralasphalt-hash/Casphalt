Our Story

Central Asphalt Sealcoating is located in Lake Mary, FL and we proudly serve Central Florida, including but not limited to, Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Volusia, Lake, and Marion Counties.

We are a full service pavement maintenance company. Central Asphalt Sealcoating is family owned and operated and we provide a one-stop-shop for all of your parking lot needs. In addition to asphalt and sealcoating services, we offer some concrete work, including sidewalk repair for our existing customers.

We’ve been around for over 34 years, so we’re experts at what we do, and we work with commercial and residential customers. We only used the highest quality materials, like Jennite, and all of out technicians are manufacturer approved and trained.

Our mission is to provide superior quality pavement products and services to area businesses at competitive prices. Central Asphalt Sealcoating does this by offering a complete line of products and services to meet your exact needs.

Ready to get started? We offer free estimates and can’t wait to see how we can help you! Give us a call today, we can’t wait to meet you! In our world, everything really is black and white or has yellow stripes!
