Budgeting

Annual Parking Lot Budget Template

A practical way to schedule crack sealing, sealcoating, and capital work.

April 2, 2025 • 4–6 min read

The 3–5 Year Cycle

  • Annual: Crack sealing + inspections.
  • Every 3–5 years: Sealcoat + restripe.
  • As needed: Cut-out patches; overlay when fatigue cracking spreads.

Template columns to track

  • Area name / square footage
  • Condition notes & photos
  • Crack sealing (year/budget)
  • Sealcoat (year/budget)
  • Striping & ADA (year/budget)
  • Repairs/overlay (year/budget)

Tips for smoother projects

  1. Bundle work: Sealcoat + stripe in one mobilization where possible.
  2. Phase access: Keep a portion of parking open; schedule overnight if needed.
  3. Communicate early: Post notices and traffic plans for tenants.
  4. Document results: Keep photos and as-builts for next cycle.
